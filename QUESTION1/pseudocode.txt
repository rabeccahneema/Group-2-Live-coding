Input: A list of projects (each with required skills), a list of teams (each with team members, their skills, preferences, and availability).
Output: A list showing which team is assigned to which project, considering skills, preferences, and availability.

Steps:

1. Start by listing all the projects and their required skills.

2. List all the teams along with each member's skills, preferences, and availability.

3. Sort the projects by importance or urgency (optional, based on goal).

4. For each project, go through the list of teams.

5. Check if a team has all the skills needed for the project.
 
6. Also check if the team is available and prefers that kind of project.

7. If multiple teams match, choose the one with the strongest match (best skill fit and preference).

8. Assign the project to the selected team and mark them as busy.
